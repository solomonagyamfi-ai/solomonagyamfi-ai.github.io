# HR & Operations Reporting

> **Professional application** · Excel · Power BI · People and operations analytics

## Business brief

Use recurring workforce and operational records to create clearer management summaries for monitoring, discussion and planning.

## Reporting scope

The work covers areas such as:

- attendance patterns
- employee satisfaction responses
- workforce attrition
- fleet and fuel monitoring
- vehicle servicing and maintenance
- recurring monthly and quarterly reporting

## Analytical process

1. Reviewed source records for completeness and consistency.
2. Standardised categories, reporting periods and definitions.
3. Calculated relevant workforce and operational indicators.
4. Compared changes across months or quarters.
5. Summarised patterns, implications and follow-up actions for management review.

## Key challenge

Operational data can be fragmented, inconsistently recorded and sensitive. The analysis therefore requires controlled definitions, careful validation and strong confidentiality practices.

## Business outcome

The work supports clearer workforce discussions, cost monitoring and operational planning by turning routine records into more structured, decision-ready summaries.

## Data ethics

Employer records, individual employee information and confidential financial values are intentionally excluded from this public portfolio. Any future demonstration files should use de-identified or synthetic data.

## Recommended public repository contents

- synthetic sample dataset
- data dictionary
- documented calculations
- anonymised dashboard screenshots
- methodology and limitations note
